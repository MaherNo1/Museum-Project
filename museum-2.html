    THE
    EGYPTIAN MUSEUM

    The Egyptian Museum in Cairo (EMC) is the oldest archaeological museum
    in the Middle East,housing over 170,000 artefacts.
    It has the largest collection of Pharaonic antiquities in the world.
    The Museum's exhibits span the Pre-Dynastic Period till the Graeco-Roman Era (c. 5500 BC - AD 364).

    "The museum has an impressive collection, but the layout could be improved. Some of the labels were missing or hard to read, and it felt a bit crowded. Still, it's worth a visit if you're into ancient history."
