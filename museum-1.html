    Welcome to the NMEC


    The National Museum of Egyptian Civilization (NMEC) is a large
    museum located in Old Cairo, a district of Cairo, Egypt.
    Partially opened in 2017, the museum was officially inaugurated
    on 3 April 2021, with the moving of 22 mummies, including 18 kings
    and four queens, from the Egyptian Museum in central Cairo,
    in an event termed the Pharaohs' Golden Parade.
    The museum displays a collection of 50,000 artifacts,
    presenting the Egyptian civilization from
    prehistoric times to the Modern era.
